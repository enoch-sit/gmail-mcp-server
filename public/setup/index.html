Step 1 — OAuth2 Credentials

Enter your Google Cloud OAuth2 client credentials. Go to Google Cloud Console → Create Credentials → OAuth 2.0 Client ID (Desktop app).

Add http://localhost:3000/oauth/callback as an Authorized redirect URI.

Step 2 — Permission Scopes

Choose the level of access the MCP server will have to your Gmail account.

Step 3 — Authorize with Google

Click the button below to open the Google authorization page. Sign in with the Gmail account you want to use, then grant the requested permissions. You'll be redirected back here automatically.
